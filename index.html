What is Pomodoro?


The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo.

It uses a timer to break work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, followed by a 5 minute break.
These intervals are called "pomodoros" and after 4 "pomodoros" completed you take a 15 minute break.
